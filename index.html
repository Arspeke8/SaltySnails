The environment as we know and love.

The life that we lead and the lifestyle choices that we make heavily depend on where we are in the world, more specifically the environment in which we live. It provides resources used to build homes and keep us warm, resources to power our cars or start fires that keep us warm at night. All of these things are incredibly important and most of which we rely on heavily every day. Beyond what it has to offer The environment is important beyond just life for us it supports animals, bugs and plants. That is just one of the many reasons that it is vital we take care of it. Today the world is facing a problem of overuse and abuse of nature and all that it has to offer leads to very damaging situations for its inhabitants including us! That is just one of the many reasons it is of absolute importance that we take care of the places in which we live and the impact we leave on the world around us. Many in recent years have stepped up to try and help preserve and protect the environment such as Salty The Snail! However Salty and many out there just like him need your help!

How else can you Help Salty and do your part?

Taking on the task of helping the environment can seem a bit overwhelming at first but Salty has many suggestions on how you can help. There are many things you man not even realize you can change in your normal everyday life in order to reduce your footprint and do a little good here are just a few of those things



  • Replace old non energy efficient lights around your home
  • Reuse any leftover water while doing things like cooking ECT
  • Use regular towels to dry your hands and dishes instead of paper ones
  • Always eat leftovers!
  • Start a garden or plant and care for plants!
  • Switch from disposable silverware and water bottles to a more reusable alternative
  • Take shorter showers at nightime to reduce water usage limit yourself to a progressively shorter shower
  • Shop with reusable bags
  • Make sure to turn off lights when exiting a room and leave TVs and other appliances off while not in use
  • Use public transportation such as buses or trains

There are so many more ways to help your environment there are just a few. Hopefully you can see how just a few tweaks in daily life can drastically affect your usage and footprint on your environment. Try out our checklist below where you can log all your pro environment goals!
